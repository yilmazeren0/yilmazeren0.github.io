Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers about how DevCollab works

What is DevCollab?

DevCollab is a developer collaboration platform to create and join open-source projects, participate in events, and more.

How can I create a project?

You need to log in and go to the Projects page. Click 'Create New Project' and fill the form to launch your idea.

Is joining events free?

Most events are free. Some may require a reservation, which you can do through the Events page.

How does the chat feature work?

After joining a project, you can access real-time chat with other contributors from the Chat section.
